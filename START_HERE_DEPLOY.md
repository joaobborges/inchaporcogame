**Time: 30 seconds | Cost: FREE | Temporary URL**

**Why ngrok?**
- ✅ Instant deployment
- ✅ No account needed (free tier)
- ✅ Perfect for tonight's game
- ⚠️ URL changes each time

**Quick Steps:**
1. Download ngrok: https://ngrok.com/download
2. Run `python3 server.py` in one terminal
